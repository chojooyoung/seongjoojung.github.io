About me

I'm a 3rd year Ph.D. Student in University of Minnesota, in Profs. Birol and Daunenhauer Groups.

I graduated Seoul National University in Seoul, Korea with B.S. degree in Chemical Engineering and a minor degree in Computer Science.

I study fundamentals of heterogeneous catalysis using density functional theory, tackling material science problems along the way.
